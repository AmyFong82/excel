Services By Area

  • Alliance Health – Mecklenburg County (Charlotte)

    1. Peer Support (PSS) – Support provided by individuals with lived experience in mental health or substance use recovery to help others navigate their recovery journey.

    2. Outpatient Therapy (OPT) – Individual and group counseling services for mental health and substance use disorders.

    3. Medication Management (Med) – Psychiatric evaluations and medication oversight to ensure effective mental health treatment.

    4. Substance Abuse Intensive Outpatient Program (SAIOP) – PA structured program including group therapy, individual counseling, and recovery support for individuals struggling with substance use.

    5. Individual and Transitional Support – One-on-one assistance tailored to personal development and recovery goals. Services that assist individuals in transitioning from higher levels of care (such as inpatient or residential programs) to independent living or community-based support.

  • Vaya Health – Salisbury, Shelby, (Rowan)

    1. Peer Support (PSS) – Recovery-focused support provided by peers with lived experience in mental health and substance use challenges.

    2. Outpatient Therapy (OPT) – Therapeutic services, including individual and group counseling, to support mental health and substance use treatment.

    3. Medication Management (Med) – Psychiatric evaluations and medication oversight to ensure effective mental health treatment.

  • Partners Health Management

    Gastonia, Concord, Kannapolis, Monroe, Albemarle, Huntersville, Mt. Holly

    1. Peer Support (PSS) – Peer-led services offering guidance and recovery support.

    2. Medication Management (Med) – Psychiatric services to manage medications for individuals with mental health conditions.

    3. Targeted Case Management (TCM) – A structured approach to coordinating care and connecting individuals to resources.

    4. Psychosocial Rehabilitation (PSR) – A program designed to develop social, vocational, and independent living skills.

    5. Outpatient Therapy (OPT) – Therapy and counseling services tailored to mental health and substance use needs.

    6. Substance Abuse Intensive Outpatient Program (SAIOP) – A structured outpatient program designed for individuals in recovery from substance use disorders.

    7. Community Support Team (CST) – Intensive, community-based treatment services for individuals with severe and persistent mental illness.

    8. Intensive In Home (IIH) – Higher-level behavioral health treatment, including crisis intervention, residential care, or other specialized supports.

  • Trillium Health Resources

    • Contracted Services Only – Trillium does not provide direct services but contracts with providers to offer behavioral health and intellectual/developmental disability (I/DD) services.
